# 05-areas — Life Areas

**Purpose:** Ongoing life areas that never "complete" — tracked, reviewed, and updated over time.

## What goes here

- Standing commitments and responsibilities: health, finance, relationships, learning
- Business area summaries (high-level, not project-specific)
- Personal brand and career positioning
- Each area is a single .md file, not a folder — it's a living document

## What doesn't go here

- Active projects → `02-projects/`
- Reference material about the area → `04-resources/`
- Goals tied to an area → `06-goals/`

## Standard area files

| File | Covers |
|---|---|
| `personal-brand.md` | Online presence, content strategy, positioning |
| `finance-personal.md` | Personal budget, savings, spending patterns |
| `finance-business.md` | Business revenue, burn, financial summaries |
| `health-fitness.md` | Exercise routines, nutrition, energy patterns |
| `learning.md` | Skills being developed, courses, reading list |
| `relationships.md` | Family, partner, meaningful connections |
| `wellbeing.md` | Mental health, rest, stress patterns |

## Area note structure

```markdown
---
type: area
created: YYYY-MM-DD
modified: YYYY-MM-DD
tags: [area, health-fitness]
status: active
---

# [Area Name]

## Current state
[2-3 sentences on where things stand]

## What I'm tracking
- [metric or habit]

## Recent updates
- YYYY-MM-DD — [what changed]

## Links
- [[relevant permanent notes]]
- [[related goals]]
```

## Update cadence

- Areas are reviewed during the monthly consolidation
- Updated when something significant changes — not daily
- Financial areas: updated monthly with summary figures, not per-transaction
